## Negative / Failure Scoring

Every chunk also receives negative flags for:
- Violations or near-violations of HAIST principles
- Failures of RACK-informed consent
- Unwanted Grok guardrail behaviors (emotional distancing, over-caution, immersion breaking)
- ADHD-related issues (topic pivot context loss, poor statefulness)
- Low-quality or repetitive content

Negative scoring is used for awareness and boundary enforcement, **not** for fine-tuning on harm.
